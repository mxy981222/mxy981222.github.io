Self-analysis

Strengths:

- I am a patient and persistent person. When I met some difficulty, I will try my best to tackle it no matter how long it takes.
- I am rational. I seldom make decisions based on my emotion, instead, I can step out of my feelings and do things objectively.
- I am also optimistic. Once when I was a sophomore with low GPA, I cheered myself up in a short period and did my best to study hard and gradually obtained a high GPA at the end.

Weaknesses:

- I lack self-discipline. I always play video games till late night rather than sleep earlier even though I know it is bad to my health.
- I am not good at communication and making friends. I never attend parties and I do not have much friends. And I am also afraid of talking to others.

As a Meng ECE student aiming to work as a Software Engineer, I believe my strengths can let me develop great technical skills, which is an essential aspect to become a Software Engineer. However, I still need to work on improving my self-discipline and communication skills, since these aspects can help me in the long run, not only as a Software Engineer but also a healthy person.

External analysis

My family and friends said that I am a kind person and dedicate in everything I determined to do. They also said that I have to sleep earlier because a healthy body is important in the long run. Moreover, they told me that sometimes I could be very strict on them, when they said something wrong even if it is just a detail, I will say a lot to tell them they are wrong.

Congruency analysis

According to self-analysis and external analysis, we all agree on that I am persistent and dedicated. However, my family and friends also told me that I am harsh on them. After thinking though it, I think it is because I am a rational person and I treat myself and them in the same way. In this way, I believe I am too rational and can bother my family and friends. This is the first time that I understand that rationality can give others a hard time. I will work hard to improve on this.

Feature-Benefit Model

-Rationality => can find the best solution for a problem and won't make decision with emotion.
-Persistent => can have a high productivity and learning speed.
-patient => can focus on one thing for a longtime and make full use of time, which can increase productivity.
-Optimistic => can handle difficulties in a good manner.
